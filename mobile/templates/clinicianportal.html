Dictum Health Clinician Portal

A medical portal to facilitate remote doctor-patient communication and record keeping.

Patient vital signs are displayed in real-time and rendered in diagnostic charts. Live video calling is supported via the WebRTC platform. Other features include DICOM (x-ray, CAT scan, etc) viewing, patient alert management, and visual trending of patient data.

Supported (and visualized) medical tests include height/weight, temperature, ECG, blood pressure, pulse oximetry, audiogram, and spirometry.
JavaScript, jQuery, WebRTC, FlotCharts, Chart.js, Java
